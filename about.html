Who We Are

VERA Interiors was founded with a singular vision: to bring world-class interior design to Sri Lanka. We specialize in transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary environments that inspire and comfort. From luxury apartments in Colombo to boutique restaurants, our portfolio reflects our commitment to excellence.

Our team of passionate designers and architects works closely with every client to understand their unique needs and aesthetic preferences. We don't just decorate; we curate experiences tailored to your lifestyle.

"Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works."
